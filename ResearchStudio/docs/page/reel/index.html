Automating the last mile of research dissemination.

Drop in one PDF and get back the artifacts that turn a paper into a conference submission and a public release: a structured asset bundle, a print-ready poster, a narrated walkthrough video, and a bilingual blog post — ready for an editor.

  • 4 composable skills
  • 1 shared extraction
  • 3 publication artifacts

Skills

Composable, reusable.

🧱

Paper2Assets

Upstream extraction · run once per paper

A poster-agnostic <outdir>/ — full text, cleaned figure rasters, captions, metadata, a 9-section paper_spec.md, institute logos and paper/code QR codes, plus a canonical sections.json + narration.json + manifest.json package.

  • Poppler
  • PyMuPDF
  • LLM crop review
🎨

Paper2Poster

ICML · NeurIPS · CVPR · ACL · NAACL

A 60×36 in landscape poster (HTML + PDF + PNG), or an A0 portrait variant for ACL-style venues, with per-section narration audio. A measured fill loop keeps every section reading FULL.

  • HTML→PDF
  • 6 templates
  • html2pptx
🎬

Paper2Video

Lightning talks · virtual recordings

A narrated MP4 walkthrough of the paper. Orchestrates ppt-master for slides, Azure OpenAI TTS for narration, and ffmpeg for compositing. 1080p @ 30 fps by default.

  • ppt-master
  • Azure TTS
  • ffmpeg
✍️

Paper2Blog

Bilingual outreach in one pass

Two editor-ready Word documents: a Chinese WeChat article (blog_cn.docx) and an English research blog (blog_en.docx) — sharing one evidence map and one set of figures, voiced natively for each audience.

  • python-docx
  • WeChat
  • Editor-ready
